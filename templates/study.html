How to Study More in Less Time

Smart study techniques to save time and boost performance.

Set SMART Goals

Be clear on what to learn, why, and by when.

Active Recall

Test yourself by asking questions and answering without looking at notes.

Spaced Repetition

Use flashcards and review key concepts at intervals .

Feynman Technique

Explain what you learned in simple language to someone else or on paper.

Pomodoro (25/5)

Focus for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break; after 4 rounds, take a longer break.

Consistency Beats Intensity

Study 1–2 hours every day instead of cramming all in one night.

Remove Distractions

Turn off notifications, keep your phone silent, and block focused study time.

Keep Notes Short

Write down only key points, formulas, and examples in concise format.

Rest & Exercise

Sleep 7–8 hours and do light exercise to boost memory and focus.
