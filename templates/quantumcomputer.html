Quantum Computer

The future of ultra-fast computing

What is a Quantum Computer?

A quantum computer is a special type of computer that uses quantum mechanics principles to process information. Instead of using bits (0 or 1), it uses qubits, which can be both 0 and 1 at the same time.

This allows quantum computers to solve complex problems much faster than traditional computers.

How Does a Quantum Computer Work?

Qubits

Qubits can exist in multiple states at once using superposition.

Entanglement

Qubits can be connected so that changes in one affect the other instantly.

Quantum Gates

Quantum gates manipulate qubits to perform calculations.
