Become an AI Engineer

Learn how to build intelligent systems and work in Artificial Intelligence

Who is an AI Engineer?

An AI Engineer designs, develops, and deploys artificial intelligence systems that can solve real-world problems. They work with machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.

AI Engineers help businesses automate processes, make predictions, and build smart applications.

Learning Roadmap

1 Programming Basics

Learn Python, Git, and basic data structures.

2 Math & Statistics

Linear Algebra, Probability, Statistics, Calculus.

3 Machine Learning

Supervised, Unsupervised Learning, Scikit-Learn.

4 Deep Learning

Neural Networks, TensorFlow, PyTorch.

5 NLP & CV

Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, OpenCV.

6 Projects & Deployment

Build real AI projects, deploy using Flask/Django or Cloud.
