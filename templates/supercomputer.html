Super Computer

The fastest and most powerful computers in the world

What is a Super Computer?

A supercomputer is a very powerful computer that can perform millions or billions of calculations per second. It is used for complex tasks like weather forecasting, space research, artificial intelligence, and scientific simulations.

Supercomputers use thousands of processors working together to solve extremely large problems quickly.

Key Features

Extreme Speed

Performs trillions of calculations per second.

Massive Processing Power

Thousands of CPUs and GPUs work together.

Large Memory

Stores huge amounts of data for fast processing.

Uses of Super Computers

Weather Forecasting
Space Research
Medical Research
Artificial Intelligence
